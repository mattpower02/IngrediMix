Creating an Account

Step 1

First, input a valid email that you would like your IngrediMix account to be associated with. Next input your password into the password section AND input your password into the confirmed password section. Then tap/click on the "Sign Up" button.

Step 2

Now that you've entered your account details, IngrediMix has sent you a confirmation email (See Image). Click on the link provided in the email to verify your email with IngrediMix. Now that your email is verified you can now sign into your IngrediMix Account.

Search for a Recipe

Step 1

In the text box, input the ingredients you would like to cook with (there is no limit!). Then, input your desired cooking time in minutes, e.g. 15 minutes, 30 minutes, 60 minutes. Optionally you can insert any allergies you have so that they are not used in the recipe generation.

Step 2

Congrats! You have successfully searched for a recipe. Now all you need to do is follow the given recipe and enjoy your meal!

Save a Recipe

Step 1

If you fell in love with the recipe you generated, you can save it! First, click on a save recipe button of your choice.

Step 2

After selecting your save recipe slot, name the recipe whatever you like and click save. Congrats! You have successfully saved a recipe!
